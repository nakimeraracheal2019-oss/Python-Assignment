📌 What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity involves protecting computer systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access, attacks, and damage.

⚠️ Common Cyber Attacks

Phishing, malware, ransomware, and social engineering are some of the most common cyber threats today.

🧪 Digital Forensics Investigation

Digital forensics involves collecting, preserving, and analyzing digital evidence to investigate cybercrimes.

  • Evidence Collection
  • Data Analysis
  • Timeline Reconstruction
  • Reporting Findings

🛡️ How to Stay Safe

Always use strong passwords, enable two-factor authentication, and avoid suspicious links or downloads.
